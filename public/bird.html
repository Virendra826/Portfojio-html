🐦 Bird – The Feathered Flyer

  1. Birds have hollow bones that make them light enough to fly in the sky.
  2. Not all birds can fly — penguins swim and ostriches run fast instead!
  3. They use their beaks instead of teeth to eat, peck, and build nests
  4. Parrots are clever birds — some can talk and copy human voices!
  5. Birds lay eggs and keep them warm until baby chicks hatch. 🐣
