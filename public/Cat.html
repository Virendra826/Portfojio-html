🐱 Cat – The Curious Explorer

  1. Cats can jump up to 6 times their body length in one leap!
  2. Their whiskers help them feel spaces — like built-in sensors.
  3. They sleep for 12–16 hours a day to save energy for pouncing.
  4. Cats purr when they’re happy, but also to calm themselves.
